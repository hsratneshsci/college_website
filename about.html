About us

Sairam Polytechnic College is a renowned institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in the year 1995, the college has been providing quality technical education to students for over two decades. The college is affiliated with the Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Tamil Nadu, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. Sairam Polytechnic College offers a wide range of diploma courses in engineering and technology, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, Computer Science, and Information Technology. The college has a team of experienced faculty members who provide world-class education to students, and state-of-the-art infrastructure with modern laboratories and well-equipped classrooms. With its focus on academic excellence and holistic development of students, Sairam Polytechnic College has emerged as a top choice among aspiring engineers in the region.
